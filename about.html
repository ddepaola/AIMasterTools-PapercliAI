Process

How We Test & Rate AI Tools

Every review follows the same framework so you can compare tools apples-to-apples.

🔍

1. Feature Deep-Dive

We map every feature, integration, and workflow. No feature goes untested.

🎯

2. Real Task Testing

We run standardized tasks across writing, coding, research, and creative work to measure real output quality.

💰

3. Pricing Analysis

We break down every plan, calculate cost-per-use, and compare value across competitors at each price tier.

⛳

4. Structured Scoring

Each tool is scored on Output Quality, Ease of Use, Value, Speed, and Support — then combined into an overall rating.

🔁

5. Ongoing Updates

We monitor changelogs and revisit reviews whenever tools release significant updates.

📞

6. Reader Feedback

We incorporate reader experience reports to catch blind spots and real-world issues our testing might miss.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on AIMasterTools are affiliate links. If you click and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the lights on and continue producing free, independent reviews.

Importantly: affiliate relationships never influence our ratings or editorial opinions. If a tool scores low, we say so — regardless of whether we have an affiliate deal. Our goal is your trust, not a quick commission.
