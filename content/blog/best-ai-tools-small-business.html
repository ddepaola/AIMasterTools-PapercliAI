Best AI for Business Automation

Automation is where small businesses see the fastest ROI from AI. Stop doing manually what a well-configured workflow can handle 24/7.

Zapier AI

Describe your automation in plain English and Zapier builds it. "When a lead submits my contact form, add them to my CRM, send a welcome email, and notify Slack" — done in minutes. For non-technical owners, this is genuinely transformative. Plans from $20/month for 750 tasks.

Tidio (AI Customer Service)

Tidio's Lyro AI resolves up to 70% of customer questions automatically — trained on your FAQ and product content. The free plan handles 50 conversations/month, enough to prove the ROI before committing. At $25/month, it's among the best value investments a small business can make.
