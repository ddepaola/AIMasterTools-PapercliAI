⚡ 3 Quick AI Tips This Week

Use these right now.

📌 Tip #1: Use ChatGPT's “Custom Instructions” for faster outputs

Go to ChatGPT → Settings → Custom Instructions. Tell it your role, your preferred output format, and what to avoid. You’ll get better first drafts without repeating context every session. Works across all your conversations automatically.

📌 Tip #2: Perplexity beats Google for research with sources

Next time you need to research a topic, try Perplexity AI instead of Google. It cites every source inline, summarizes the key points, and lets you ask follow-up questions without losing context. The free tier is excellent for daily research tasks.

📌 Tip #3: Claude is now better than ChatGPT for long documents

Claude 3.5 Sonnet has a 200K token context window — that’s roughly 150,000 words. Drop an entire book, legal contract, or codebase into it and ask questions. For deep document analysis, it’s outperforming GPT-4 in our tests. Read our Claude review.
