Yes. The Free plan is free forever with no credit card required. You get 3 signal alerts per day on the top 10 coins. Upgrade any time when you want more power.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel Pro or Enterprise at any time from your account settings. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period — no hidden fees, no questions asked.

How accurate are the AI signals?

Our models are trained on historical price data, on-chain metrics, and social sentiment. In internal backtests (2021–2024) our Pro signals showed a positive expectancy in trending markets. Past performance does not guarantee future results — always manage your risk appropriately.

What exchanges and coins are supported?

Free covers the top 10 coins by market cap. Pro expands to 200+ assets across Binance, Coinbase, Kraken, and OKX. Enterprise covers all listed assets including altcoins and perpetual futures.

Do you offer refunds?

If you're not happy in the first 7 days of a paid plan, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no hassle.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes — switch to annual billing and save 20%. Pro drops to $23/mo ($276/yr) and Enterprise to $79/mo ($948/yr). Toggle "Annual" at the top of this page to see updated prices.

What is the Enterprise custom model fine-tuning?

Enterprise customers can provide their own historical trade data. Our team fine-tunes a signal model specifically on your strategy's patterns, improving signal relevance for your specific trading style and timeframes.
