    How It Works

    6 Signals Analyzed Simultaneously

    1

    Sentence Uniformity

    AI models produce sentences with strikingly similar lengths and rhythms. We measure variance to flag unnatural uniformity.

    2

    Transition Overuse

    Phrases like "Furthermore," "Moreover," and "It is worth noting" appear at far higher rates in AI text than human writing.

    3

    Vocabulary Diversity

    We measure type-token ratio — AI tends toward broader vocabulary than most natural writers, creating detectable spread.

    4

    Hedging Language

    AI models over-use hedging phrases ("It is important to," "One could argue") to stay neutral and comprehensive.

    5

    Passive Voice Rate

    AI writing skews toward passive constructions. High passive voice rates combined with other signals strengthen the verdict.

    6

    Filler Phrase Density

    AI text is dense with hollow connectors — "In conclusion," "As mentioned above," "It is essential." We count them all.
